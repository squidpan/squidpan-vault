# What is it

Electra and ==Pulse Data== refer to financial data management service offered by the company Gresham Technologies.

## Pulse Data (formerly Electra Data)

Pulse Data is an industry-leading service that automates the collection, aggregation, and normalization of financial data for institutional investment firms, such as asset managers, hedge funds, and fund administrators.Â 

- **Function:** It connects to over 6,000 unique data feeds from more than 1,600 global sources (including custodians, prime brokers, and broker-dealers) to consolidate securities, cash position, and transaction information.
- **Purpose:** The service helps firms reduce operational risk, improve efficiency, and support various middle and back-office workflows like reconciliation, trade matching, settlement, and client fee billing.
- **Acquisition:** The original company, Electra Information Systems, was acquired by Gresham Technologies, and its data service was rebranded as Pulse Data.
